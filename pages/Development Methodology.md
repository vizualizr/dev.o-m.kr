- ## Summary
	- What is the best software development methodology for IT service solopreneur?
	- ### Restrictions
		- The service stands upon information technology both in authoring and maintainaing.
		- Thus, only 3 hours or less is allowed for software development.
		- Agent-aided coding plays an essential role in this methodology.
		- The service owner must have a higher expertise in the core dev stack than the agent. Else the service owner will spend more time on regulating the ai generated code than guiding the development direction of AI agent.
